    Wiki Links

    Wiki links are the internal links that connect the files in your knowledge base.

    To create a wiki link use [[ and type the name of another file in your repo, for example [[graph-visualization]]

    You can also create a [[placeholder]]. A placeholder is a wiki link that doesn't have a target file. They can still be helpful to highlight connections. Open the graph with Foam: Show Graph command, and look at the placeholder node.
